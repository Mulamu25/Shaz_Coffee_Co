My Story

SHAZ COFFEE CO is a conceptual coffee shop designed to provide high-quality coffee and a relaxing environment for customers. The brand is inspired by modern cafe culture, focusing on comfort, community, and premium beverages. It aims to create a welcoming space where people can meet, work, or unwind while enjoying freshly brewed coffee.

How SHAZ Was Born: The idea for SHAZ COFFEE CO. came from a simple belief that everyone deserves a place where they feel at home. After months of dreaming and planning, Sharonah decided to turn that vision into reality. What started as a small idea grew into a warm, welcoming space where the community could gather, connect, and enjoy exceptional coffee. Today, SHAZ COFFEE CO. stands as a testament to hard work, passion, and the love of great coffee.


"Great coffee, good vibes, and a place that feels like home. That's the SHAZ promise. Come as a customer, leave as Family."

- Sharonah, Owner of SHAZ COFFEE CO.

Our Mission

At Shaz Coffee Co, our mission is to craft high-quality coffee and deliver exceptional service while creating a warm, welcoming space that feels like home for every customer.

Our Vision

To expand SHAZ COFFEE CO into a recognized brand known for premium coffee, outstanding service, and a unique customer experience.

Who We Serve

  • Students and young adults - A comfortable place to study and relax
  • Working professionals - Quick, quality coffee for busy schedules
  • Coffee lovers and casual visitors - Premium beverages for every taste
